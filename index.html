Our Mission

Empowering the Next Generation of Musicians

Too many aspiring musicians are held back — not by lack of talent, but by lack of access. Without instruments, guidance, or community, their potential goes unrealized.

Joy Music Foundation exists to change that. We provide direct resources to musicians who need them most: instruments to play, mentors to learn from, and a community to grow with. We believe that when you remove barriers, extraordinary music follows.

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, every dollar donated goes directly toward building a world where musical talent knows no economic limits.

What We Do

Our Programs

Instrument Access

We provide instruments to young musicians who cannot afford them, ensuring that no student is ever held back by lack of equipment.

Mentorship Network

We connect aspiring musicians with experienced mentors who provide personalized guidance, feedback, and professional development support.

Educational Resources

Free workshops, masterclasses, and learning materials covering music theory, production, composition, and performance skills.

Community Building

Creating spaces where musicians collaborate, perform, and grow together through events, showcases, and online platforms.

The Founder

Tiffany Joy Cuaresma

TIFFVNYJOY

A graduate of Rice University's Shepherd School of Music in classical composition, Tiffany Joy Cuaresma is a pioneer of what she calls "EDM Orchestra" — a genre-defying fusion of symphony orchestra and electronic dance music that bridges the concert hall and the festival stage.

As a signed artist on Blanco y Negro, her music streams globally on Tomorrowland Radio. She has been commissioned by the Austin Symphony Orchestra and Cincinnati Symphony Orchestra, and her film scores have screened at OUTFEST and the Burbank International Film Festival.

Tiffany founded Joy Music Foundation to give back to the community that shaped her — ensuring that the next generation of musicians has the instruments, mentorship, and opportunities they need to create without limits.
